### TYPE 4 with .json
## Pack
PackMakerLite.exe --create_cs four --pack four
PackMakerLite.exe --create_cs root --pack root
## Unpack
PackMakerLite.exe --load_cs four --unpack four

# --parallel --nolog


### TYPE 4 without .json
## Pack
PackMakerLite.exe --forcetype 4 -p four
## Unpack
PackMakerLite.exe -u four
